Todd's recipe for Teriyaki-chicken marinade

Ingredients
	1/ 2 kg chicken tenderloins
	1/4 cup vegetable stock
	1 tea spoon ginger
	1 stick / bunch lemon grass (discard the woody bit)
	1/2 cup soy sauce (kunis manis)
	1 teaspoon garlic

Mix together leave to marinate over night. The chicken
turns this lovely dark brown/black colour right through
the meat. Delicious!
